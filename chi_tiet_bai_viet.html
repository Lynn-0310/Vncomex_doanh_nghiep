Normally, people will choose to come to Sapa around September and October. If you only travel to Fansipan normally, you can come in October - December. At this time, the weather is not too cold. This is also the right time to see the whole of Sapa from above as well as "hunt" for clouds here.

Sapa in the morning is usually a bit chilly, the fog is not too dense. At noon, the weather becomes warmer because of the addition of golden sunshine, visitors can participate in other fun and entertainment activities.

However, the weather here is quite erratic, so you should be mentally prepared for weather changes here. Please consider more about when is the most suitable time to travel to Fansipan!
There are two popular ways that self-sufficient Fansipan tourists choose to travel to the top of Fansipan: taking the cable car or climbing the mountain. Either form will bring you extremely interesting and memorable experiences.

If you are a sports lover, love adventure and want to be conquered, climbing is for you. To ensure greater safety during travel, this form has been more limited than before. However, for those who are passionate about climbing and have adventurous interests, this has never been a hot hobby.

For those who do not have climbing experience and are not in good health, taking the cable car will be a safe and appropriate way to get to the top of Fansipan. A safe experience does not mean it is not interesting, you will be able to see the panoramic view from above, take a glimpse of the majestic mountains, lush green forests and billions of other interesting things that will bring to you. you a memorable experience.

Ga cáp treo cách trung tâm thành phố khoảng 3km. Bạn có thể đi bằng taxi hoặc đi bộ đến. Đi taxi mất tầm 10 phút và đi bộ thì khoảng 30 phút. Du khách có thể đi từ nhà thờ Đá theo đường của Thác Bạc rồi đến đến Nguyễn Chí Thanh. Sau đó đi thẳng đến khu du lịch Fansipan Legend. Vì quãng đường không dài cộng thêm có nhiều bản chỉ dẫn rất dễ đi dễ tìm nên du khách ít khi gặp khó khăn gì khi đến ga cáp treo cả.

Xuất phát từ khách sạn MGallery ở trung tâm Sapa, tàu đi với tốc độ 10m/s, mỗi giờ có 2000 khách lên xuống tàu. Đây là hình thức di chuyển tiết kiệm thời gian và tiền bạc hơn so với tự đi ô tô lên đỉnh núi rất nguy hiểm và khó đi. Giá vé tàu hỏa Mường Hoa là 100.000 VNĐ/ người, vừa rẻ vừa tiện lợi nên được rất nhiều du khách lựa chọn.
Sau khi đi cáp treo đến ga Fansipan, du khách sẽ phải mất tầm 20 đến 30 phút để leo bộ 600 bậc thang nữa mới đến đỉnh Fansipan. Du khách cũng có thể lựa chọn đi thêm 1 chuyến tàu ngắn nữa trong suốt hành trình tiếp theo của mình.

The cable car station is about 3km from the city center. You can go by taxi or walk there. Taking a taxi takes about 10 minutes and walking is about 30 minutes. Visitors can go from the Stone Church along the path of Thac Bac and then to Nguyen Chi Thanh. Then go straight to Fansipan Legend tourist area. Because the distance is not long and there are many easy-to-find instructions, visitors rarely have any trouble getting to the cable car station.

Departing from MGallery hotel in central Sapa, the train travels at a speed of 10m/s, with 2,000 passengers boarding and disembarking every hour. This is a form of transportation that saves time and money compared to going by car to the top of the mountain, which is very dangerous and difficult. Muong Hoa train ticket price is 100,000 VND/person, which is both cheap and convenient, so it is chosen by many tourists.
After taking the cable car to Fansipan station, it will take visitors about 20 to 30 minutes to climb another 600 steps to reach the top of Fansipan. Visitors can also choose to take another short train ride during their next journey.
