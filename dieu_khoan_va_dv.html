About cookies and web beacons: Cookies are data folders that are stored temporarily or permanently in the hard drive of the Client’s computer. Cookies are used to verify, track (protect status) and maintain specific information about use and users of the Website Vncomex, such as preferences for the website. Cookie folders may also be placed on the Client’s computer by advertising service providers that have entered into an Agreement with Vncomex for the above purposes and the data collected by these cookies is completely invalid. Customers who do not agree may delete all cookies already located on the hard drive of the computer by searching the folder with “cookie” in its name and deleting it. In the future, customers may edit browser choices so that cookies are blocked. Please note that: If you do so, you may not be able to use the full features of theVncomex.
